apricot a juicy, soft fruit, resembling a small peach, of an orange-yellow color.
bread food made of flour, water, and yeast or another leavening agent, mixed together and baked.
carrot a tapering orange-colored root eaten as a vegetable.
doughnut a small fried cake of sweetened dough, typically in the shape of a ball or ring.
eclair a small, soft, log-shaped pastry filled with cream and typically topped with chocolate icing.
fondue a dish in which small pieces of food are dipped into a hot sauce or a hot cooking medium such as oil or broth.
